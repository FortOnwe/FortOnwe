### About me

- 🎓 Pursuing an **M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science** at the **University of Hull, UK**

- 🔬 Research interests: **LLM infrastructure & retrieval systems (RAG)**, **data-centric AI / MLOps**, and **domain-applied NLP** for regulated, high-stakes verticals

- ✍️ Manuscript in preparation — *A reliability-first fraud detection serving stack*: SLO-aware cascade selection, conformal abstention, and continuous calibration that reduces **ECE@20 from 0.3393 → 0.0077**, with rolling ECE and Brier monitoring

- 🧪 Undergraduate thesis: *Banking Fraud Detection Using Machine Learning* — PCA + SMOTE pipeline, best model Accuracy **0.9994**, F1 **0.798** (awarded top grade)

- 🛠️ Previously: **Technical Lead** at Tent App, **Co-Founder / CTO** at Fitness Yomallu, **Blockchain/Web3 Track Lead** at GDG on Campus — Babcock University
